Book Cover Designs

A.J. Towns, an acclaimed self-published author, commissioned me to create book cover art for her works. This collaboration involved multiple meetings to discuss the narratives and ensure the visuals captured the essence of her stories. For the artwork, I utilized Procreate, starting from initial sketches to the final polished designs. Once completed, I also used Procreate to present the book cover mockups in a professional format.

Here are two notable projects I worked on with her:

Grand Duke of Noir

This story follows a medieval baker, a single father of two young children, who falls in love with a high noble. Towns envisioned the cover illustrating the protagonist's family life with a rustic, warm tone. I designed a dynamic scene where the baker is at the forefront, busy baking, while his children are playfully causing chaos in the background—a cohesive and engaging visual narrative.

Thane’s Promise

For Towns' debut novel, *Thane’s Promise*, I created two distinct covers. The softcover features detailed character art, highlighting my illustration of the protagonist, Thane. For the hardcover, I crafted a design resembling an elegant party invitation envelope, aligning with the novel's themes of intrigue and grandeur.
